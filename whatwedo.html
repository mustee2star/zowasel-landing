Marketplace

We connect African farmers with national, regional, and global markets. We offer a platform to create offers, set prices, compare best prices, negotiate with buyers, deliver, and get paid within

24 hours. Our commodity trading platform connects tens of thousands of farmer cooperatives, merchants, and startups with vetted processors and manufacturers to earn more crops, maximize profit, and minimize risk.

Market Report

We collaborate with farmers, cooperatives, processors, wholesalers, aggregators, manufacturers, retailers, and commodity marketing experts across value chains to provide unparalleled price discovery and supply chain planning services to save time and money.

Alternative Finance for Farmers

Zowasel bridges the GAP between farmers and financial institution using alternative credit evaluation scoring system or (ACCESS) to unlock access to credit and lending in a low risk and cost efective system.

The platform that helps financial service providers to identify and quantify the risks and opportunities with agricultural credit across value chain.

Partnering for Regenerative Agriculture

Food systems are becoming increasingly unbalanced. With the increasing population and growth in the demand for food, our ability to supply it is outpacing. Across Africa, smallholder farmers continue to practice old and unsustainable farming systems.

Zowasel API Gateway

Zowasel API is a fully managed service that makes it easy to deploy Zowasel's service at any scale offering plug and play integration that saves you time and money.

By tapping into the zowasel API, startups and agribusinesses instantly access a treasure trove of agricultural value chain solutions and services without reinventing the wheel or building from scratch.

The zowasel API is available via RESTFUL and WebSocket APIs and enables real-time two-way communication applications. The API Seamlessly integrates with all Zowasel channels and delivers real-time data insights, monitoring, access and control at your fingertips
