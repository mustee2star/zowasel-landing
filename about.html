Simplifying Tomorrow!

We leverage technology and data science to improve farmers' productivity, sustainability, and profitability from knowledge, finance, and seed to market.

Zowasel works with corporate organizations across industries, from agriculture sciences to finance and manufacturing to brewing, that are committed to the environment to transform agriculture from a carbon source to a carbon sink.

Our flagship commodity marketing platform connects tens of thousands of processors and manufacturers to farmers, cooperatives, and merchants to buy and sell crops, food ingredients, feeds, and agrochemicals to save time and money.

Our Alternative Credit Evaluation Scoring System (ACESS), Measurement, Reporting, and Verification (MRV), farm-to-factory Traceability technologies, and last-mile solutions solve farmers’ challenges across value chains.
